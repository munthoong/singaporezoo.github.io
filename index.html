About Singapore Zoo

Opened in 1973, Singapore Zoo is located at Mandai Wildlife Reserve spanning 26 hectares. Singapore Zoo is dedicated to national and regional conservation projects and is one of the country's designated centres for rescued wildlife.

Animal Zones

  • Wild Africa

    • Enjoy the sights of the largest, tallest and fastest animals on land at Wild Africa!
    Get the opportunity to enjoy encounters with wildlife: Giraffe and White Rhino Feeding for an up-close exciting experience!
    For rates and timings, click here.




  • Fragile Forest

    • At this 20,000 cubic metre biodome that mimics a tropical rainforest, we have:

    - Black-and-white ruffed lemur
    - Toco toucan
    - Tree nymph butterfly
    - Golden lion tamarin

    ... and many more!


  • Great Rift Valley of Ethiopia

    • Home to the Hamadryas baboons and Nubian ibexes , explore further and discover many more animals along with the recreated Konso and Amharic villages!
